What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Education

Education as the key to succeed in life, under this organization we have co-joined with other well wishers and other like-minded organizations to support education for children living with Albinism in Kenya through paying school fees for them, purchase long sleeves uniforms, huts and sunny glasses in order to protect them from direct sun while at school.

Economic Empowerment Programme

Furthermore, we have gone extra-mile in bringing people with Albinism together and train them an economic activities so that they can come-up with income generating projects to support their living hood through self-employments since obtaining white colour job in Kenya is very rare.

Health Care Programe

In this programme on health matters, we have managed to enlighten them on how to maintain their skin health from high sensitivity of direct sun which can cause skin cancer due to too much light exposure. To extend our hands in this case, we have been supported them with free lotion, huts and sunny glasses.
